# Case-commons method

## The question

The commons does not ask, “How large a lawsuit can we advertise?”

It asks:

> Which exact exercise of public power is evidenced, which legal limit may
> apply, what did a court actually decide, what remedy followed, and what money
> —if any—did that remedy legally affect?

## Money has states

Keep these apart:

- **amount demanded** — a body asked for it;
- **amount paid** — evidence shows it moved;
- **amount affected** — a decision or remedy touched it;
- **gross recovery** — a legal basis supports return or damages before
  deductions;
- **net recovery** — what remains after fees, funding, tax, costs and other
  liabilities;
- **contingent exposure** — it might have become payable but did not;
- **unknown** — the public record cannot establish it.

Zero is always an allowed scenario. No release publishes a guessed probability
or “expected value”.

## A remedy is not automatically money

Quashing can remove a decision and require reconsideration. A declaration can
state the legal position. A mandatory or prohibiting order can require or stop
action. None of those automatically creates damages.

Any money claim needs its own legal basis, evidence of loss or payment,
causation, a live route, a limitation analysis and a complete deduction record.

## Decentralised without becoming a rumour market

Anyone can mirror a complete public packet. The packet is source-resolving,
versioned and carries a content fingerprint. A corrected or external fork
should identify its predecessor digest. Version 1 still relies on repository
history and does not claim to provide a signed lineage ledger.

The public release decision binds the exact canonical corpus digest, corpus
version and reviewed case IDs. A later edit cannot inherit an earlier approval.

Private material stays outside the commons. A qualified professional can fill a
local assessment template, verify their status at the official register and
share it through a channel agreed with the prospective client. TaxSorted does
not choose, rank, introduce, contact or hold the matter.

## Off-switch

`UK_CASE_COMMONS_EMERGENCY_STOP=true` closes case packets at the API origin and
those responses require revalidation before reuse. Previously downloaded copies
cannot be recalled. The separately deployed static human pages require their
own rollback. The method, schemas and source boundary remain available for
inspection. `UK_CASE_COMMONS_STOPPED_CASE_IDS` can hide one stable case ID; an
invalid value fails closed for the case surface without stopping unrelated tax
routes. A publication rollback never alters a private matter file because
TaxSorted does not hold one.
