## Filing Frequency & Period Reference

| Filing | Frequency | Period Covered | Deadline Formula |
|--------|-----------|----------------|------------------|
| **SA100** | Annual | Tax year (6 Apr - 5 Apr) | 31 Jan following tax year |
| **SA800** | Annual | Tax year (6 Apr - 5 Apr) | 31 Jan following tax year |
| **SA900** | Annual | Tax year (6 Apr - 5 Apr) | 31 Jan following tax year |
| **CT600** | Annual | Accounting period (max 12 months) | 12 months after period end |
| **VAT100** | Quarterly | VAT quarter (3 months) | Period end + 1 month + 7 days |
| **VAT100** | Monthly | VAT month (optional) | Month end + 1 month + 7 days |
| **VAT100** | Annual | Annual accounting scheme | 2 months after year end |
| **PAYE FPS** | Per payday | Pay period | On or before payday |
| **PAYE EPS** | Monthly | Month | 19th of following month |
| **P11D** | Annual | Tax year (6 Apr - 5 Apr) | 6 July following tax year |
| **P60** | Annual | Tax year (6 Apr - 5 Apr) | 31 May following tax year |
| **CH Accounts** | Annual | Accounting period | 9 months (private) / 6 months (public) |
| **CS01** | Annual | Review period (12 months) | 14 days after review date |
| **CC Annual Return** | Annual | Financial year | 10 months after FYE |
| **FCA Annual Return** | Annual | Financial year | 7 months after FYE |
| **CIC Report** | Annual | Accounting period | With CH accounts |
| **TRS Update** | Annual | Calendar year (if SA900) | 31 January |
| **MTD ITSA** | Quarterly | Tax year quarters | 5th of 2nd month after quarter |

## Detailed Filing Requirements by Entity

### Individual (Employed Only)

| Filing | Required? | Frequency | Period | Deadline |
|--------|-----------|-----------|--------|----------|
| **SA100** | Only if criteria met* | Annual | Tax year (6 Apr - 5 Apr) | 31 January |

*Criteria: income >£150k, rental income, capital gains, high income child benefit charge, etc.

---

### Individual (Self-Employed)

| Filing | Required? | Frequency | Period | Deadline |
|--------|-----------|-----------|--------|----------|
| **SA100** | Yes | Annual | Tax year (6 Apr - 5 Apr) | 31 January (online) |
| **VAT100** | If turnover >£90k | Quarterly | 3-month VAT periods | Period + 37 days |
| **PAYE/RTI** | If employees | Per payday | Pay period | On/before payday |
| **CIS** | If contractor | Monthly | Calendar month | 19th following |
| **MTD ITSA** | From Apr 2026 (>£50k) | Quarterly | Tax year quarters | Quarterly + EOPS |

## Filing Calendar Cross-Reference

### January Deadlines

| Date | Filing | Entity Types |
|------|--------|--------------|
| **31 Jan** | SA100 | Individuals |
| **31 Jan** | SA800 | Partnerships, LLPs |
| **31 Jan** | SA900 | Trusts |
| **31 Jan** | TRS Update | Trusts (if SA900) |

### Quarterly (VAT)

| Quarter End | Filing | All VAT-registered |
|-------------|--------|-------------------|
| **+37 days** | VAT100 | Companies, Partnerships, Individuals |

| Event | Filing | Entity Types |

| **ARD +12m** | CT600 | Companies |
