# Charity Accounts: Filing Requirements

> **Authority**: Charity Commission for England and Wales
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Charity Accounts and Trustees' Annual Report |
| **Purpose** | Public record of charity finances and activities |
| **Who Files** | Registered charities (income thresholds apply) |
| **Frequency** | Annual |
| **Method** | Online via Charity Commission portal |

---

## Who Must File Accounts

### Income Thresholds

| Charity Income | Accounts Required? | Where Filed? |
|----------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Under £10,000** | No | N/A |
| **£10,000 - £25,000** | Recommended | Optional |
| **£25,000 - £1m** | Yes | Charity Commission |
| **Over £1m** | Yes + Audit | Charity Commission |

### Charitable Companies (Dual Filing)

| Regulator | What to File | Deadline |
|-----------|--------------|----------|
| **Companies House** | Statutory accounts | 9 months after ARD |
| **Charity Commission** | Accounts + AR | 10 months after FYE |

---

## Accounts Format

### By Income Level

| Income | Format Options | Audit Requirement |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Under £250,000** | Receipts & Payments OR Accruals | No (independent examination if £25k+) |
| **£250,000 - £1m** | Accruals (Charities SORP) | Independent examination |
| **Over £1m** | Accruals (Charities SORP) | Full audit required |

### Receipts & Payments Accounts

Simple format showing:
- Cash received during year
- Cash paid during year
- Assets and liabilities at year end

### Accruals Accounts (SORP)

| Component | Content |
|-----------|---------|
| **SOFA** | Statement of Financial Activities |
| **Balance Sheet** | Assets, liabilities, funds |
| **Notes** | Accounting policies, fund analysis |
| **Cash Flow** | If income >£500k |

---

## Charities SORP

### Statement of Recommended Practice

| SORP Version | Applies To |
|--------------|------------|
| **FRS 102 SORP** | Larger charities (>£500k income) |
| **FRS 102 SORP (1A)** | Smaller charities (£250k-£500k) |
| **FRSSE SORP** | Withdrawn - no longer valid |

### SORP Requirements

| Section | What's Included |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Objectives** | Charity's purposes |
| **Activities** | What charity does |
| **Achievements** | Impact and outcomes |
| **Financial review** | Reserves policy, investments |
| **Plans** | Future activities |
| **Governance** | Trustee structure |

---

## Trustees' Annual Report

### Mandatory Content (All Charities)

| Section | Required Information |
|---------|---------------------|
| **Reference details** | Charity name, number, address |
| **Trustees** | Names during year |
| **Advisers** | Bankers, auditors, solicitors |
| **Objectives** | Charitable purposes |
| **Activities** | What charity did |
| **Achievements** | Outcomes and impact |

### Additional Content (£500k+ Income)

| Section | Required Information |
|---------|---------------------|
| **Financial review** | Reserves policy, investment policy |
| **Principal risks** | Risk management approach |
| **Plans** | Future developments |
| **Related parties** | Transactions, relationships |
| **Remuneration** | Staff pay policy |

### Additional Content (£1m+ Income)

| Section | Required Information |
|---------|---------------------|
| **Governance** | Board structure, decision-making |
| **Fundraising** | Methods, approach, complaints |
| **Grant-making** | Policies, decisions |
| **Social investments** | If applicable |

---

## Independent Examination

### When Required

| Income | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| **Under £25,000** | Not required |
| **£25,000 - £250,000** | Independent examination |
| **£250,000 - £1m** | Independent examination (qualified) |
| **Over £1m** | Full audit |

### Examiner Qualifications

| Income Level | Examiner Must Be |
|--------------|------------------|
| **£25k - £250k** | Reasonably competent person |
| **£250k - £1m** | ACCA, ICAEW, ICAS member, or equivalent |

---

## Filing Deadline

### 10 Months After Financial Year End

| Financial Year End | Deadline |
|-------------------|----------|
| 31 March 2026 | 31 January 2027 |
| 31 December 2025 | 31 October 2026 |
| 30 June 2026 | 30 April 2027 |

### Filed With Annual Return

Accounts submitted as part of Annual Return process.

---

## How to File

### Online Only

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Portal** | Charity Commission online services |
| **Format** | PDF upload (usually) |
| **Account** | Registered charity contact |
| **Fee** | Free |

---

## CIO Accounts

### Charitable Incorporated Organisations

Same requirements as other charities:
- File with Charity Commission only
- No Companies House filing
- Same income thresholds apply
- Same SORP requirements

---

## Excepted Charities

### Not Required to Register

Some charities excepted from registration but may still need accounts:
- Armed forces charities
- Some religious charities
- Scout/Guide groups (under £100k)

If voluntarily registered, normal rules apply.

---

## Group Accounts

### When Required

| Situation | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Charity controls subsidiary** | Consolidated accounts |
| **Material subsidiaries** | Parent + consolidated |
| **Immaterial subsidiaries** | Notes disclosure only |

---

## Failure to File

### Consequences

| Stage | Action |
|-------|--------|
| **Warning** | Reminder from CC |
| **Default** | Marked on register |
| **Continued failure** | Inquiry may be opened |
| **Persistent failure** | Trustees may face action |

No financial penalties, but:
- Affects charity's reputation
- May impact funding applications
- CC can open statutory inquiry

---

## Public Access

All filed accounts are publicly available:
- On Charity Commission website
- Searchable by charity name/number
- Includes Trustees' Annual Report
