# Annual Accounts: Companies House Filing

> **Authority**: Companies House
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Annual Accounts and Reports |
| **Purpose** | Public record of company's financial position |
| **Who Files** | All companies (with exceptions) |
| **Frequency** | Annual (per accounting reference period) |
| **Method** | Online (iXBRL) or Paper |

---

## Who Must File

| Entity Type | File Accounts? | File at CH? |
|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| **Private limited** | Yes | Yes |
| **Public limited** | Yes | Yes |
| **LLP** | Yes | Yes |
| **CIC** | Yes (+ CIC Report) | Yes |
| **Charitable company** | Yes | Yes (+ Charity Commission) |
| **CIO** | Yes | No (CC only) |
| **Limited partnership** | Varies | Generally no |
| **General partnership** | No | No |

---

## Filing Deadline

### Private Companies

**9 months** after accounting reference date (ARD).

| ARD | Filing Deadline |
|-----|-----------------|
| 31 March 2026 | 31 December 2026 |
| 31 December 2025 | 30 September 2026 |
| 30 June 2026 | 31 March 2027 |

### Public Companies

**6 months** after accounting reference date.

### New Companies (First Accounts)

**21 months** from incorporation OR 3 months from ARD (whichever is longer).

## Types of Accounts

### Size Thresholds (2024-25)

| Category | Turnover | Balance Sheet | Employees |
|----------|----------|---------------|-----------|
| **Micro** | ≤£632,000 | ≤£316,000 | ≤10 |
| **Small** | ≤£10.2m | ≤£5.1m | ≤50 |
| **Medium** | ≤£36m | ≤£18m | ≤250 |
| **Large** | >Medium | >Medium | >250 |

Must meet 2 of 3 criteria for 2 consecutive years.

### What Each Must File

| Category | Balance Sheet | P&L | Notes | Directors' Report | Audit |
|----------|---------------|-----|-------|-------------------|-------|
| **Micro** | Abbreviated | No | Minimal | No | No |
| **Small** | Abbreviated | No | Reduced | Yes | Usually no |
| **Medium** | Full | Abbreviated | Full | Yes | Yes |
| **Large** | Full | Full | Full | Yes | Yes |

## Dormant Companies

### Definition

Company with **no significant accounting transactions** except:
- Filing fees
- Penalty payments
- Shares paid on incorporation

### Dormant Company Accounts

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Form** | AA02 (abbreviated balance sheet) |
| **Audit** | Not required |
| **Notes** | Minimal |
| **Fee** | Standard filing fees |

## How to File

### Online (Recommended)

| Method | Accepted Formats |
|--------|------------------|
| **WebFiling** | Simple accounts via CH portal |
| **Software filing** | iXBRL tagged accounts |
| **HMRC joint filing** | CT600 + accounts together |

**Fee**: Free (online)

### Paper

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Form** | Printed accounts |
| **Fee** | £33 (additional to online) |
| **Send to** | Companies House, Cardiff |

## iXBRL Tagging

### Mandatory for

- All accounts filed online
- Part of CT600 submission to HMRC

### What's Tagged

- Balance sheet items
- P&L items
- Directors' report elements
- Key notes

### Accepted Taxonomies

- UK GAAP (FRS 101, 102, 105)
- IFRS (for qualifying companies)

---

## Penalties

### Late Filing (Fixed Penalties)

| Delay | Private Company | Public Company |
|-------|-----------------|----------------|
| **≤1 month** | £150 | £750 |
| **1-3 months** | £375 | £1,500 |
| **3-6 months** | £750 | £3,000 |
| **>6 months** | £1,500 | £7,500 |

### Repeat Offenders

Penalties **doubled** if accounts were late in previous year.

### Criminal Liability

Directors can be prosecuted for persistent failure.

---

## Group Accounts

### When Required

Parent company must file consolidated accounts if:
- It has subsidiary undertakings, AND
- Group exceeds small company thresholds

### Exemptions

- Small groups (all small companies)
- Parent included in larger group accounts

---

## Audit Requirements

### Audit Exempt

Small companies meeting thresholds may be audit exempt unless:
- Subsidiary of non-small group
- Public company
- Regulated (bank, insurance)
- Shareholders holding 10%+ request audit

### Audit Required

- Medium and large companies
- Public companies
- Regulated entities
- Groups exceeding thresholds

## Charity Accounts

### Dual Filing

Charitable companies must file:
1. **Companies House**: Statutory accounts
2. **Charity Commission**: Annual return + accounts
