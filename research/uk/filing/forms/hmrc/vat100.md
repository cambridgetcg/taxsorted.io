# VAT100: VAT Return

> **Form Number**: VAT100
> **Authority**: HMRC
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Value Added Tax Return |
| **Purpose** | Report VAT collected and reclaim VAT paid |
| **Who Files** | All VAT-registered businesses |
| **Frequency** | Quarterly (usually) or Monthly/Annual |
| **Method** | MTD software (mandatory from April 2022) |

---

## Who Must File

### VAT Registration Threshold

| Threshold | Amount (2024-25) |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Compulsory** | Taxable turnover >£90,000 (last 12 months) |
| **Compulsory** | Expected >£90,000 in next 30 days |
| **Voluntary** | Any turnover (choose to register) |

### Entities That Can Register

| Entity | Can Register? |
|--------|---------------|
| Sole trader | Yes |
| Partnership | Yes (in partnership name) |
| Limited company | Yes |
| LLP | Yes |
| Charity | Yes |
| Trust | Yes (if trading) |
| Club/Association | Yes |

---

## VAT Return Boxes

| Box | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Box 1** | VAT due on sales |
| **Box 2** | VAT due on acquisitions from EU |
| **Box 3** | Total VAT due (Box 1 + Box 2) |
| **Box 4** | VAT reclaimed on purchases |
| **Box 5** | Net VAT (Box 3 - Box 4) - pay or reclaim |
| **Box 6** | Total sales (excluding VAT) |
| **Box 7** | Total purchases (excluding VAT) |
| **Box 8** | Total supplies to EU (goods) |
| **Box 9** | Total acquisitions from EU (goods) |

## Deadline

### Standard Quarterly Returns

**1 month + 7 days** after VAT period end.

| VAT Quarter End | Deadline |
|-----------------|----------|
| 31 March | 7 May |
| 30 June | 7 August |
| 30 September | 7 November |
| 31 December | 7 February |

### Payment Deadline

Same as filing deadline (for Direct Debit: 3 working days later).

### Annual Accounting Scheme

| Event | Deadline |
|-------|----------|
| Interim payments | 9 monthly payments |
| Final return | 2 months after year end |

---

## How to File

### Making Tax Digital (MTD)

**Mandatory for all VAT-registered businesses** from April 2022.

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Software** | MTD-compatible software required |
| **Digital links** | Data must flow digitally (no manual re-entry) |
| **Bridging software** | Allowed (connects spreadsheet to HMRC) |

### Exempt from MTD

- Businesses run by those who are digitally excluded
- Religious objection
- Remote location without internet

---

## VAT Schemes

### Flat Rate Scheme

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Turnover limit** | ≤£150,000 (excluding VAT) |
| **How it works** | Pay fixed % of turnover |
| **No input VAT reclaim** | Simplified calculation |

### Cash Accounting Scheme

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Turnover limit** | ≤£1.35m |
| **How it works** | Account for VAT when paid/received |
| **Benefit** | Cash flow help |

### Annual Accounting Scheme

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Turnover limit** | ≤£1.35m |
| **How it works** | One annual return |
| **Payments** | 9 monthly instalments |

## Penalties (From January 2023)

### New Points-Based System (FA 2021)

**Late Submission Penalty**:

| Submission Frequency | Penalty Threshold |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| Annual | 2 points |
| Quarterly | 4 points |
| Monthly | 5 points |

- 1 point per late submission
- At threshold: £200 penalty per late return
- Points expire after 24 months of compliance

### Late Payment Penalties

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **1-15 days** | None |
| **16-30 days** | 2% of VAT outstanding at day 15 |
| **31+ days** | Additional 2% of VAT outstanding at day 30 |
| **Ongoing** | 4% per year (daily) |

### Interest

**From day 1**: Bank Rate + 2.5%

---

## Common VAT Rates

| Rate | Applies To |
|------|------------|
| **20%** (Standard) | Most goods and services |
| **5%** (Reduced) | Home energy, child car seats |
| **0%** (Zero) | Food, children's clothes, books |
| **Exempt** | Insurance, education, health |
| **Outside scope** | Wages, dividends, non-business |

---

## Partial Exemption

If business makes both taxable and exempt supplies:
- Can only reclaim VAT on taxable activities
- Must calculate "recoverable proportion"
- De minimis limit: <£625/month exempt input tax

## Group VAT Registration

Companies under common control can register as VAT group:
- One return for whole group
- Intra-group supplies ignored
- Representative member files
