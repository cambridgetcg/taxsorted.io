# SA100: Self Assessment Tax Return

> **Form Number**: SA100
> **Authority**: HMRC
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Self Assessment Tax Return |
| **Purpose** | Report all income, gains, and claim reliefs for individuals |
| **Who Files** | Self-employed, directors, high earners, those with complex tax affairs |
| **Frequency** | Annual (per tax year: 6 April - 5 April) |
| **Method** | Online (Government Gateway) or Paper |

---

## Who Must File SA100

### Mandatory Filing (TMA 1970 s.7)

| Situation | Threshold |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Self-employment income** | Any (unless <£1,000 trading allowance) |
| **Partnership income** | Any |
| **Untaxed income** | >£2,500 |
| **Total income** | >£150,000 |
| **Company director** | Usually (unless only PAYE, no benefits) |
| **Capital gains** | Above annual exempt (£3,000 for 2024-25) |
| **High Income Child Benefit** | Income >£60,000 + receiving Child Benefit |
| **Foreign income** | Any taxable foreign income |
| **Rental income** | >£2,500 (or any loss claim) |
| **Trust income** | Taxable trust distributions |
| **HMRC notice** | If HMRC issues notice to file |

### Not Required

- Employed only with PAYE (no other income)
- State pension only (below threshold)
- Income below personal allowance (no other factors)

## Deadlines

### Filing Deadlines

| Method | Deadline | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Paper** | 31 October | Following tax year end |
| **Online** | 31 January | Following tax year end |

**Example for 2025-26 Tax Year**:
- Tax year: 6 April 2025 - 5 April 2026
- Paper deadline: 31 October 2026
- Online deadline: 31 January 2027

### Payment Deadlines

| Payment | Deadline | Amount |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **Balancing payment** | 31 January | Tax owed minus POAs |
| **First POA** | 31 January | 50% of previous year's liability |
| **Second POA** | 31 July | 50% of previous year's liability |

**POA** = Payment on Account (required if tax liability >£1,000 and <80% collected at source)

## Penalties

### Late Filing (TMA 1970 Sch.55)

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **1 day late** | £100 (fixed) |
| **3 months late** | £10/day for up to 90 days (max £900) |
| **6 months late** | Greater of £300 or 5% of tax due |
| **12 months late** | Greater of £300 or 5% of tax due |

**Maximum**: £1,600 + percentage penalties

### Late Payment (FA 2009 Sch.56)

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **30 days** | 5% of tax unpaid |
| **6 months** | Additional 5% |
| **12 months** | Additional 5% |

**Total potential**: 15% of tax due + interest

### Interest

**Current rate**: Bank Rate + 2.5% = **7.00%** (as of January 2026)

Runs from due date until payment.

## MTD for Income Tax (from April 2026)

From April 2026, individuals with self-employment/property income >£50,000 must:
- Use MTD-compatible software
- Submit quarterly updates
- Submit End of Period Statement (EOPS)
- Submit Final Declaration (replaces SA100)

See: [MTD ITSA](../../mtd/mtd-requirements.md)
