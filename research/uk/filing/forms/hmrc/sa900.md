# SA900: Trust and Estate Tax Return

> **Form Number**: SA900
> **Authority**: HMRC
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Trust and Estate Tax Return |
| **Purpose** | Report income and gains for trusts and estates |
| **Who Files** | Trustees of most trusts; personal representatives |
| **Frequency** | Annual (per tax year) |
| **Method** | Online or Paper |

---

## Who Must File

### Trusts That Must File

| Trust Type | Required? | Notes |
|------------|-----------|-------|
| **Discretionary trust** | Yes | Income taxed at trust rates |
| **Interest in possession** | Yes | Even though beneficiary taxed |
| **Accumulation trust** | Yes | Income accumulated |
| **Mixed trust** | Yes | Multiple interest types |
| **Charitable trust** | Sometimes | If trading income |
| **Bare trust** | **No** | Beneficiary files (transparent) |

### Estates

Personal representatives must file SA900 for:
- Year of death
- Administration period (until estate distributed)

### Exemptions (No SA900 Required)

- **Bare trusts** - Beneficiary reports income
- **Registered pension schemes** - Exempt
- **ISA managers** - Exempt
- **Trusts with income <£100** - May be exempt (check conditions)

---

## Trust Tax Rates (2025-26)

| Income Type | Rate |
|-------------|------|
| **Standard rate band** (first £1,000) | 20% (basic) / 8.75% (dividend) |
| **Trust rate** (above £1,000) | 45% |
| **Dividend trust rate** | 39.35% |

## Deadline

### Filing Deadline

| Method | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| **Paper** | 31 October following tax year |
| **Online** | 31 January following tax year |

- Tax year: 6 April 2025 - 5 April 2026
- Paper: 31 October 2026
- Online: 31 January 2027

| Payment | Deadline |
|---------|----------|

## Trust Registration Service (TRS)

### Must Register Before Filing

Since 2022, **all UK express trusts** must register with TRS:
- Registration deadline: 90 days from creation
- Annual update: Within 90 days of changes
- Required before SA900 can be filed online

### Exceptions (No TRS Registration)

- Bare trusts (still register, but simpler)
- Charitable trusts (registered with CC)
- Will trusts (first 2 years)
- Pension trusts

See: [TRS Registration](../other-regulators/trs.md)

## Penalties

### Late Filing

Same structure as SA100:

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **1 day late** | £100 |
| **3 months** | £10/day for 90 days |
| **6 months** | 5% of tax or £300 |
| **12 months** | Additional 5% or £300 |

### Late Payment

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **30 days** | 5% of tax |
| **6 months** | Additional 5% |
| **12 months** | Additional 5% |

Plus interest at Bank Rate + 2.5%.

## Distributions to Beneficiaries

### Tax Pool and R185

When trust distributes income:

1. **Trust pays tax** at trust rates
2. **Adds to "tax pool"** - record of tax paid
3. **Distributes net income** to beneficiary
4. **Issues R185 certificate** showing:
   - Gross income
   - Tax deducted (from pool)
5. **Beneficiary** includes on their SA100
   - Credits tax already paid via pool

### If Pool Exhausted

If trust distributes more than pool covers:
- Trust pays additional 45% tax on distribution
- Beneficiary still gets credit for full 45%
