# SA800: Partnership Tax Return

> **Form Number**: SA800
> **Authority**: HMRC
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Partnership Tax Return |
| **Purpose** | Report partnership income, allocate profits to partners |
| **Who Files** | All partnerships (general, limited, LLPs) |
| **Frequency** | Annual (per accounting period) |
| **Method** | Online or Paper |

---

## Who Must File

### All Partnerships

| Entity Type | Required? |
|-------------|-----------|
| **General Partnership** | Yes |
| **Limited Partnership (LP)** | Yes |
| **Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)** | Yes |
| **Scottish Partnership** | Yes |

**Filed by**: Nominated partner (or precedent partner if none nominated)

### What It Reports

- Total partnership income (trading, property, investment)
- Allocation of profits/losses to each partner
- Capital allowances claimed
- Partnership expenses

**Note**: SA800 does NOT calculate tax - partners report their share on SA104 and pay individually.

## Supplementary Pages

| Form | Name | When Required |
|------|------|---------------|
| **SA800PS** | Partnership Statement | Always (partner allocations) |
| **SA801** | Partnership trading (short) | Simple trading <£85k |
| **SA802** | Partnership trading (full) | Complex or ≥£85k |
| **SA803** | Partnership UK property | Rental income |
| **SA804** | Partnership foreign | Foreign income |

---

## Deadline

### Filing Deadline

| Method | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| **Paper** | 31 October following tax year |
| **Online** | 31 January following tax year |

**Based on**: Tax year in which partnership's accounting period ends.

### Example

Partnership accounting period: 1 July 2025 - 30 June 2026
- Falls in tax year 2026-27 (ends after 5 April 2026)
- Paper deadline: 31 October 2027
- Online deadline: 31 January 2028

## Partner's Responsibilities

Each partner must also:

1. **File SA100** - Personal Self Assessment return
2. **Include SA104** - Partnership supplement (their share)
3. **Pay their own tax** - Partnership doesn't pay

### SA104: Partnership Supplement

| Form | When Used |
|------|-----------|
| **SA104S** (Short) | Simple profit share |
| **SA104F** (Full) | Complex (losses, capital gains, foreign) |

---

## Penalties

### Late Filing (Partnership)

Same as individual SA:

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **1 day late** | £100 per partner (max £300 × partners) |
| **3 months** | £10/day per partner for 90 days |
| **6 months** | 5% of tax or £300 per partner |
| **12 months** | Additional 5% or £300 per partner |

**Note**: Penalties multiply by number of partners at penalty date.

### Partners' Individual Penalties

Partners also face penalties if their SA100/SA104 is late.

---

## Special Cases

### LLP: Salaried Members

If LLP has salaried members (ITTOIA 2005 ss.863A-G):
- Salaried members treated as employees
- LLP operates PAYE on their "salary"
- Remaining members still use SA800/SA104

### Limited Partnership: Loss Restriction

Limited partners:
- Can only claim losses up to capital contribution
- ITA 2007 ss.103A-D applies
- Document contribution on SA104

### Mixed Membership Partnership

If partnership has corporate members:
- Anti-avoidance rules apply (ITA 2007 ss.850A-C)
- Corporate member files CT600 (not SA104)

## Information Required

### Partnership Statement (SA800PS)

For each partner:
| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Name | Partner's name |
| UTR | Partner's Unique Taxpayer Reference |
| NI Number | If individual |
| Share of profit | £ amount |
| Share of loss | £ amount (if applicable) |
| Capital gains | Share allocated |

### Trading Pages (SA801/802)

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Turnover | Gross income |
| Expenses | Allowable deductions |
| Capital allowances | AIA, WDA claimed |
| Net profit | For allocation |
