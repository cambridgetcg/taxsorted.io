# PAYE & RTI: Employer Filing Requirements

> **Authority**: HMRC
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Pay As You Earn / Real Time Information |
| **Purpose** | Report employee earnings and deductions |
| **Who Files** | All employers paying employees |
| **Frequency** | On or before each payday (RTI) |
| **Method** | Payroll software (mandatory) |

---

## Who Must Operate PAYE

### Required If

| Situation | Required? |
|-----------|-----------|
| Paying employees ≥ lower earnings limit (£123/week) | Yes |
| Providing benefits in kind | Yes |
| Employing someone previously unemployed | Yes |
| Employing pension recipients | Usually |

### Not Required If

- All employees earn below LEL (£123/week)
- No employees (directors only taking dividends)

---

## RTI Submissions

### Full Payment Submission (FPS)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **When** | On or before each payday |
| **Content** | Employee details, pay, tax, NI |
| **How** | Payroll software → HMRC |

**FPS Contents**:
- Employee name, NI number, date of birth
- Gross pay this period
- Tax deducted this period
- NI contributions (employee and employer)
- Student loan deductions
- Pension contributions
- Year-to-date figures

### Employer Payment Summary (EPS)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **When** | Monthly (by 19th) if adjustments |
| **Content** | Recovery of statutory payments, CIS deductions |
| **Also used for** | Reporting no employees paid in month |

**EPS Used For**:
- Statutory maternity/paternity/sick pay recovery
- CIS deductions suffered
- Apprenticeship levy
- Employment allowance claim
- "No payment" months

### Earlier Year Update (EYU)

For corrections to previous tax year after final submission.

## Key Deadlines

### Monthly/Weekly Cycle

| Event | Deadline |
|-------|----------|
| **FPS** | On or before payday |
| **Tax/NI payment (electronic)** | 22nd of following month |
| **Tax/NI payment (post)** | 19th of following month |
| **EPS** | 19th of following month |

### Year-End

| Event | Deadline |
|-------|----------|
| **Final FPS** | On/before last payday of tax year |
| **Final EPS** | 19 April (if needed) |
| **P60 to employees** | 31 May |
| **P11D submission** | 6 July |
| **P11D(b)** | 6 July |
| **Class 1A NI payment** | 22 July |

## Annual Forms

### P60: Employee Year-End Certificate

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **What** | Certificate of pay and tax for year |
| **Give to** | Each employee employed at 5 April |
| **Deadline** | 31 May following tax year |
| **Format** | Paper or electronic |

### P11D: Benefits in Kind

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **What** | Report taxable benefits provided |
| **Who** | Employees earning ≥£8,500 or directors |
| **Deadline** | 6 July following tax year |
| **Examples** | Company car, medical insurance, loans |

### P11D(b): Return of Class 1A NI

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **What** | Declaration of total Class 1A NI due |
| **Deadline** | 6 July |
| **Payment** | Class 1A NI due by 22 July |

## Penalties

### Late FPS

| Delay | Penalty (per month) |
|-------|---------------------|
| **1-50 employees** | £100 |
| **51-249** | £200 |
| **250+** | £300 |
| **Extended failure** | Additional 5% of tax/NI |

**Grace**: First late FPS in tax year = no penalty.

### Late Payment

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **1-3 defaults** | None (warning) |
| **4-6 defaults** | 1% of late payment |
| **7-9 defaults** | 2% of late payment |
| **10+ defaults** | 3% of late payment |

Plus interest at Bank Rate + 2.5%.

### Late P11D

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **Per form** | £300 per 50 employees |
| **Continued failure** | Additional penalties |

### Late P60

Fixed penalty per employee not provided.

## Employment Allowance

### Annual NI Relief

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Amount** | £5,000/year (2024-25 onwards) |
| **Eligibility** | Employers' NI liability <£100,000 |
| **Exclusions** | Single director companies, public bodies |
| **Claim** | Via EPS at start of tax year |

---

## Payrolling Benefits

### Optional Reporting

Instead of P11D, can "payroll" benefits:
- Add benefit value to taxable pay
- Tax collected via PAYE
- Register with HMRC before tax year starts

### Benefits That Can Be Payrolled

- Company cars
- Medical insurance
- Gym membership
- All except:
  - Living accommodation
  - Beneficial loans (interest-free/low)

---

## CIS (Construction Industry Scheme)

### If Contractor in Construction

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Verify** | Subcontractors via HMRC |
| **Deduct** | 20% (registered) or 30% (unregistered) |
| **Report** | Monthly CIS return by 19th |
| **Pay** | Deductions to HMRC by 22nd |

## Software Requirements

PAYE/RTI **must** be submitted via software:
- Commercial payroll software (Sage, Xero, etc.)
- HMRC Basic PAYE Tools (free, up to 10 employees)

**Paper submission not accepted**.
