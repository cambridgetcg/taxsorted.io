# CIC Report: Community Interest Company Annual Filing

> **Authority**: CIC Regulator (Office of the Regulator of Community Interest Companies)
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Community Interest Company Report |
| **Purpose** | Demonstrate community benefit activities |
| **Who Files** | All Community Interest Companies |
| **Frequency** | Annual (with accounts) |
| **Method** | Filed with Companies House |

---

## What is a CIC?

### Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Asset lock** | Assets locked for community benefit |
| **Community purpose** | Must benefit the community |
| **Limited dividends** | Dividend cap applies |
| **Not a charity** | Can make profits |
| **Company form** | Ltd by shares or guarantee |

---

## CIC Report Requirements

### Mandatory Content

| Section | What to Include |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Activities** | General description of activities |
| **Community benefit** | How activities benefited community |
| **Stakeholder engagement** | Consultation with stakeholders |
| **Directors' remuneration** | Details of any remuneration |
| **Dividends** | Amounts paid (if applicable) |
| **Asset transfers** | Any transfers at less than market value |

### Declaration

Report must include declaration that CIC has:
- Continued to satisfy community interest test
- Not engaged in excluded activities

---

## Filing Requirements

### CIC Report + Accounts

| Company Size | CIC Report | Accounts |
|--------------|------------|----------|
| **All CICs** | Yes (mandatory) | Yes |
| **Micro** | Yes | Abbreviated |
| **Small** | Yes | Abbreviated |
| **Medium/Large** | Yes | Full |

CIC Report filed WITH accounts to Companies House.

---

## Filing Deadline

### Same as Company Accounts

**9 months** after accounting reference date (private CIC).

| ARD | Filing Deadline |
|-----|-----------------|
| 31 March 2026 | 31 December 2026 |
| 31 December 2025 | 30 September 2026 |
| 30 June 2026 | 31 March 2027 |

---

## How to File

### With Companies House

| Method | Process |
|--------|---------|
| **Online** | WebFiling or software filing |
| **Paper** | Post to Companies House |
| **Fee** | Standard CH fees apply |

CIC Report submitted as attachment to annual accounts.

---

## CIC Report Form

### CIC34 (Community Interest Statement)

For ongoing annual reporting:

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Part 1** | Company details |
| **Part 2** | Activities description |
| **Part 3** | Community benefit |
| **Part 4** | Stakeholder involvement |
| **Part 5** | Directors' remuneration |
| **Part 6** | Dividends and interest |
| **Part 7** | Asset transfers |
| **Part 8** | Declaration |

---

## Directors' Remuneration

### Must Disclose

| Information | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Total remuneration** | All directors combined |
| **Highest paid** | Individual amount |
| **Justification** | Why remuneration is appropriate |

No cap on director remuneration, but must be reasonable.

---

## Dividend Cap

### If CIC Limited by Shares

| Aspect | Limit |
|--------|-------|
| **Maximum dividend** | 35% of distributable profits |
| **Per share cap** | 20% above Bank of England base rate |
| **Carried forward** | Limited carry-forward allowed |

### Performance-Related Interest

On loans or debentures:
- Maximum 20% above BoE base rate
- Must be disclosed in CIC Report

---

## Asset Lock

### What It Means

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| **Sale of assets** | Must be at full market value |
| **Transfers** | Only to asset-locked bodies |
| **On dissolution** | Assets to another asset-locked body |
| **No distribution** | To members (except dividend cap) |

### Asset-Locked Bodies

- Other CICs
- Charities
- Certain Community Benefit Societies

---

## Penalties

### Late Filing

Same as standard company late filing penalties:

| Delay | Private CIC |
|-------|-------------|
| **≤1 month** | £150 |
| **1-3 months** | £375 |
| **3-6 months** | £750 |
| **>6 months** | £1,500 |

### Failure to Include CIC Report

| Consequence | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Rejection** | Accounts rejected by CH |
| **Investigation** | CIC Regulator may investigate |
| **Deregistration** | Risk of losing CIC status |

---

## CIC Regulator Powers

### Oversight Functions

| Power | When Used |
|-------|-----------|
| **Investigate** | Concerns about compliance |
| **Audit** | Request additional information |
| **Appoint director** | If CIC not run properly |
| **Remove director** | Misconduct |
| **Transfer assets** | To another asset-locked body |
| **Wind up** | Serious breaches |

---

## Community Interest Test

### Annual Confirmation

Each year, CIC must continue to satisfy:

| Test | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **Community benefit** | Activities benefit community |
| **Reasonable person** | Would consider it beneficial |
| **Not excluded** | Not political party, etc. |

---

## Changes During Year

### Report to CIC Regulator

| Change | Notification |
|--------|--------------|
| **Name change** | Application to Regulator |
| **Objects change** | Amendment approval |
| **Conversion from CIC** | Consent required |
| **Director removal** | Record in report |

---

## Conversion

### To/From CIC

| Direction | Process |
|-----------|---------|
| **Company → CIC** | Application with community interest statement |
| **CIC → Company** | Requires Regulator consent (rare) |
| **CIC → Charity** | Requires CC and CIC consent |

---

## Exempt CIC Activities

### Cannot Be CIC If

| Activity | Status |
|----------|--------|
| **Political party** | Excluded |
| **Political campaigning** | Excluded (if primary purpose) |
| **Support for political party** | Excluded |
