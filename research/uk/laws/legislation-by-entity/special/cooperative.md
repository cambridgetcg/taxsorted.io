## Key Principle

> A co-operative is **owned and controlled by its members** for mutual benefit. It is taxed similarly to a company but with special rules for member transactions.

**Co-operative Principles**:
1. Voluntary and open membership
2. Democratic member control (one member, one vote)
3. Member economic participation
4. Autonomy and independence
5. Education, training and information
6. Co-operation among co-operatives
7. Concern for community

## Co-operative and Community Benefit Societies Act 2014

### Section 2: Registration

```
(1) A society may be registered under this Act as—
    (a) a co-operative society, or
    (b) a community benefit society.
```

### Section 2(2): Co-operative Society Definition

```
A society may be registered as a co-operative society if—
(a) it is a bona fide co-operative society, and
(b) it satisfies one of the conditions in subsection (4).
```

### "Bona Fide Co-operative" Test

FCA guidance requires:
- Member economic participation
- Democratic control
- Surplus distributed according to transactions (not investment)

### Community Benefit Society

Alternative registration for community purposes:
- Asset lock requirement
- Similar to CIC but different registrar

## Corporation Tax Treatment

### CTA 2010 Part 13: Societies

### Section 1055: Interest Payments to Members

```
A payment of interest by a registered society to a member
in respect of a share in the society is not a distribution
for the purposes of the Corporation Tax Acts.
```

**Effect**: Co-ops can deduct share interest as an expense (unlike normal dividends).

### Section 1054: Share Interest Deduction

| Aspect | Normal Company | Co-operative |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| **Dividend** | Not deductible | N/A |
| **Share interest** | N/A | **Deductible** (up to limit) |

**Limit**: Reasonable commercial rate (typically Bank Rate + margin)

## Member Patronage Dividend

### What Is It?

Distribution of surplus based on member's trading activity:

```
PATRONAGE DIVIDEND EXAMPLE

Co-op surplus: £100,000
Member A transactions: £10,000 (10% of total)
Member B transactions: £5,000 (5% of total)

Patronage dividend:
- Member A: £100,000 × 10% = £10,000
- Member B: £100,000 × 5% = £5,000
```

### Tax Treatment

| Recipient | Treatment |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Individual member** | Trading receipt (if business) or Schedule D Case VI |
| **Corporate member** | Trading receipt |
| **Co-operative** | Deductible expense |

## Registration and Regulation

### Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)

Co-ops and community benefit societies registered with FCA:

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Registration** | Apply to FCA |
| **Annual return** | FCA (not Companies House) |
| **Accounts** | File with FCA |
| **Audit** | Required above thresholds |

**Note**: NOT regulated by Companies House (though some company law applies).

### Conversion

| From | To | Permitted? |
|------|-----|-----------|
| Company → Co-op | Yes (must meet bona fide test) |
| Co-op → Company | Yes |
| Co-op → CIC | Yes |

## Share Capital Rules

### Withdrawable Shares

Unique to co-ops - shares that can be withdrawn:

| Aspect | Transferable Shares (normal) | Withdrawable Shares |
|--------|----------------------------|---------------------|
| **Transfer** | To another person | Returned to society |
| **Value** | Market value | Par value only |
| **CGT** | On disposal | No (always par value) |

### Maximum Shareholding

CCBS 2014 limits individual shareholding to promote democratic control:

**Current limit**: £100,000 per member (can be increased by Treasury order)

---

## Credit Unions

### Special Type of Co-op

Credit unions are savings and loans co-ops with additional regulation:

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Regulator** | PRA + FCA (dual regulated) |
| **Membership** | Common bond required |
| **Interest cap** | Maximum 3% per month (42.6% APR) |
| **Tax treatment** | Same as other co-ops |

**Legislation**: Credit Unions Act 1979

## Co-op vs Other Structures

| Aspect | Co-operative | CIC | Ltd Company |
|--------|-------------|-----|-------------|
| **Ownership** | Members | Shareholders | Shareholders |
| **Control** | One member one vote | Per shares | Per shares |
| **Profit distribution** | Per patronage | Capped dividend | Unlimited dividend |
| **Share interest** | Deductible | N/A | Not deductible |
| **Asset lock** | Optional | Required | No |
