# UK Filing Penalties: Comprehensive Guide

> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

This document consolidates penalty structures across UK tax and regulatory filings.

---

## HMRC Penalties

### Self Assessment (SA100, SA800, SA900)

#### Late Filing Penalties

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **1 day late** | £100 (fixed) |
| **3 months late** | +£10/day (up to 90 days = £900 max) |
| **6 months late** | +£300 or 5% of tax due (greater) |
| **12 months late** | +£300 or 5% of tax due (greater) |

**Maximum for 12+ months late**: £1,600 + 10% of tax due

#### Late Payment Penalties

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **30 days late** | 5% of tax unpaid |
| **6 months late** | +5% of tax unpaid |
| **12 months late** | +5% of tax unpaid |

**Plus interest**: Bank Rate + 2.5% (currently ~7.5% from late payment)

#### Partnerships Additional

- Partnership penalty applies to partnership
- Individual partners may face separate penalties for SA104

---

### Corporation Tax (CT600)

#### Late Filing Penalties

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **1 day late** | £100 |
| **3 months late** | +£100 (total £200) |
| **6 months late** | +10% of unpaid tax |
| **12 months late** | +10% of unpaid tax |

**Repeat offender**: If accounts late in previous period, £500 instead of £100 for first two penalties.

#### Late Payment Interest

| Component | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| **Interest** | Bank Rate + 2.5% |
| **From** | Day after due date |
| **Compounds** | Daily |

### VAT (VAT100) - New Penalty Regime (Jan 2023)

#### Late Submission Penalty (Points-Based)

| Submission Frequency | Threshold |
|---------------------|-----------|
| **Annual** | 2 points |
| **Quarterly** | 4 points |
| **Monthly** | 5 points |

- 1 point per late submission
- At threshold: £200 per further late submission
- Points expire after 24 months of compliance

#### Late Payment Penalty

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **1-15 days** | None |
| **16-30 days** | 2% of VAT outstanding at day 15 |
| **31+ days** | +2% of VAT outstanding at day 30 |
| **Ongoing** | 4% per annum (daily) |

#### Interest

| Component | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| **Late payment interest** | Bank Rate + 2.5% |
| **From** | Day 1 |

### PAYE/RTI

#### Late FPS Penalty

| Employees | Monthly Penalty |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **1-9** | £100 |
| **10-49** | £200 |
| **50-249** | £300 |
| **250+** | £400 |

**First late FPS in tax year**: No penalty (warning).

**Extended failure** (>3 months): Additional 5% of tax/NI due.

#### Late Payment Penalty

| Defaults in Year | Penalty |
|------------------|---------|
| **1-3** | No penalty (warning) |
| **4-6** | 1% of late payment |
| **7-9** | 2% of late payment |
| **10+** | 3% of late payment |

Plus interest at Bank Rate + 2.5%.

#### Late P11D

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **Per 50 employees** | £300 |
| **Continued failure** | Additional penalties |

### Failure to Notify

#### New SA Registration Required

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **Careless** | 0-30% of potential lost revenue |
| **Deliberate** | 20-70% of PLR |
| **Deliberate + concealed** | 30-100% of PLR |

## Companies House Penalties

### Late Accounts Filing (Fixed Penalties)

#### Private Companies

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **≤1 month** | £150 |
| **1-3 months** | £375 |
| **3-6 months** | £750 |
| **>6 months** | £1,500 |

#### Public Companies

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| **≤1 month** | £750 |
| **1-3 months** | £1,500 |
| **3-6 months** | £3,000 |
| **>6 months** | £7,500 |

#### Repeat Offenders

Penalties **doubled** if accounts late in previous year.

### Late Confirmation Statement

| Consequence | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Criminal offence** | Company and every officer |
| **Unlimited fine** | On prosecution |
| **Strike-off** | CH may start dissolution |
| **Director disqualification** | Possible |

## Penalty Mitigation

### HMRC

| Circumstance | Relief Available |
|--------------|------------------|
| **Reasonable excuse** | Penalty cancelled |
| **First offence** | Warning (some penalties) |
| **Voluntary disclosure** | Reduced percentage |
| **Cooperation** | Reduced penalty |

#### Reasonable Excuse Examples

- Serious illness
- Death of close relative
- HMRC error
- System failure (not user's)
- Postal delays (unexpected)

#### Not Reasonable Excuse

- Pressure of work
- Lack of information
- Mistake by agent
- Insufficient funds (for payment)

### Companies House

| Circumstance | Relief |
|--------------|--------|
| **Appeal** | Must be within time limits |
| **Exceptional circumstances** | Very limited |
| **Objection to strike-off** | Available |
