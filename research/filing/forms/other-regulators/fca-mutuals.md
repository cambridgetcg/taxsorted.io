# FCA Mutuals: Co-operative and Community Benefit Society Filing

> **Authority**: Financial Conduct Authority (Mutuals Public Register)
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Annual Return for Registered Societies |
| **Purpose** | Update public register, report financial position |
| **Who Files** | Co-operative and Community Benefit Societies |
| **Frequency** | Annual |
| **Method** | Online via FCA Connect or Paper |

---

## Who Must File

### Entities Registered with FCA Mutuals

| Entity Type | Register With | Annual Return? |
|-------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Co-operative Society** | FCA | Yes |
| **Community Benefit Society** | FCA | Yes |
| **Credit Union** | FCA (PRA dual) | Yes |
| **Friendly Society** | FCA | Yes |
| **Building Society** | FCA (PRA dual) | Separate requirements |

### Not FCA Mutuals

| Entity | Registers With |
|--------|----------------|
| Company limited by guarantee | Companies House |
| CIC | Companies House + CIC Regulator |
| CIO | Charity Commission only |
| Industrial & Provident Society (old name) | Now CBS/Co-op with FCA |

---

## Annual Return Content

### All Societies Must Report

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Society details** | Name, registered number, address |
| **Officers** | Secretary, committee members |
| **Members** | Total number of members |
| **Shares** | Share capital and withdrawable status |
| **Financial summary** | Assets, liabilities, income |

### Additional Requirements by Size

| Society Size | Additional Requirements |
|--------------|------------------------|
| **Small** (turnover <£5.6m) | Basic accounts |
| **Medium** | Full accounts |
| **Large** (turnover >£10.2m) | Full accounts + audit |

---

## Accounts Requirements

### Filing With Annual Return

| Element | Required? |
|---------|-----------|
| **Revenue account** | Yes |
| **Balance sheet** | Yes |
| **Auditor's report** | If required (see thresholds) |
| **Committee report** | Required |

### Audit Thresholds

| Threshold | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Under audit threshold** | Unaudited accounts acceptable |
| **Above audit threshold** | Must be audited |
| **Credit unions** | Different thresholds apply |

Audit exemption thresholds similar to company thresholds.

## Filing Deadline

### 7 Months After Year End

| Financial Year End | Filing Deadline |
|-------------------|-----------------|
| 31 March 2026 | 31 October 2026 |
| 31 December 2025 | 31 July 2026 |
| 30 June 2026 | 31 January 2027 |

### Credit Unions

May have different deadlines - check PRA requirements.

## How to File

### Online (FCA Connect)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Portal** | FCA Connect |
| **Account** | Society must register |
| **Submission** | Upload forms and accounts |
| **Confirmation** | Electronic acknowledgment |

### Paper

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Forms** | Standard FCA forms |
| **Send to** | FCA Mutuals, Stratford |
| **Processing** | Slower than online |

## Filing Fees

### Annual Fee Based on Assets

| Total Assets | Annual Fee (Approx) |
|--------------|---------------------|
| **Under £50,000** | ~£60 |
| **£50,000 - £100,000** | ~£120 |
| **£100,000 - £250,000** | ~£195 |
| **£250,000 - £1m** | ~£325 |
| **Over £1m** | Higher bands |

Fees reviewed annually by FCA.

---

## Penalties

### Late Filing

| Consequence | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Late fee** | Additional fee charged |
| **Warning** | FCA may contact officers |
| **Strike off** | Persistent failure may result in dissolution |
| **Officer action** | Committee members personally liable |

### Failure to File

FCA can:
- Impose sanctions
- Appoint inspector
- Cancel registration
- Prosecute officers

---

## Community Benefit Societies

### Charitable CBS

If CBS has charitable status:
- Register with both FCA and Charity Commission
- File annual return with FCA
- May need to file with CC depending on income

### Asset Lock

Community Benefit Societies typically have:
- Asset lock provisions
- Cannot distribute surplus to members
- On dissolution, assets to similar body

---

## Co-operative Societies

### Distinct Requirements

| Aspect | Co-op Requirement |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Democratic control** | One member, one vote |
| **Member benefit** | Profits distributed to members |
| **Open membership** | Generally open to join |
| **Dividends** | Based on transactions, not shares |

---

## Credit Unions

### Dual Regulation

| Regulator | Responsibility |
|-----------|----------------|
| **FCA** | Conduct regulation |
| **PRA** | Prudential regulation |

### Additional Requirements

- Quarterly returns to PRA
- Capital adequacy reporting
- Liquidity reporting
- More frequent than standard CBS

---

## Friendly Societies

### Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Incorporated** | Limited liability |
| **Unincorporated** | Personal liability |

### Additional Reporting

May need additional returns depending on activities:
- Insurance business returns
- Investment business returns
