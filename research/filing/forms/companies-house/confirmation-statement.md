# Confirmation Statement (CS01)

> **Form Number**: CS01
> **Authority**: Companies House
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-01

---

## Overview

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Full Name** | Confirmation Statement |
| **Purpose** | Confirm company information is up to date |
| **Who Files** | All companies, LLPs |
| **Frequency** | At least annually |
| **Method** | Online (WebFiling) or Paper |

---

## Who Must File

| Entity Type | Required? |
|-------------|-----------|
| **Private limited company** | Yes |
| **Public limited company** | Yes |
| **LLP** | Yes |
| **Limited partnership** | Yes |
| **CIC** | Yes |
| **Overseas company** | Yes (if UK establishment) |

**Not Required**:
- General partnerships (not registered)
- Sole traders
- Unincorporated charities

---

## What It Confirms

### Standard Information

| Section | What's Confirmed |
|---------|------------------|
| **Registered office** | Address is correct |
| **Officers** | Directors/members/secretaries |
| **SIC codes** | Nature of business |
| **Statement of capital** | Share capital details |
| **Shareholders** | PSC details correct |
| **PSC register** | People with Significant Control |

### PSC Information

Must confirm PSC register is up to date:
- Individuals with >25% shares/voting rights
- Individuals with significant control
- Relevant legal entities

---

## Deadline

### Review Period

- **Start**: Day after previous confirmation statement (or incorporation)
- **End**: 12 months later (review date)
- **Filing deadline**: 14 days after review date

### Example

| Event | Date |
|-------|------|
| Company incorporated | 1 March 2025 |
| First review period ends | 28 February 2026 |
| Filing deadline | 14 March 2026 |

### Can File Early

You can make confirmation statement at any time:
- Review period then restarts from that date
- Useful if making multiple changes

## What Changes Require Separate Filing

Confirmation statement only **confirms** - changes need separate forms:

| Change | Form |
|--------|------|
| Registered office | AD01 |
| Director appointment | AP01 |
| Director resignation | TM01 |
| Share allotment | SH01 |
| PSC change | PSC01-07 |

---

## Penalties

### Late Filing

| Consequence | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| **Criminal offence** | Company and every officer |
| **Fine** | Unlimited |
| **Strike off** | Companies House may start process |
| **Director disqualification** | Risk of disqualification |

### Striking Off

If no confirmation statement filed:
1. CH sends "First Gazette" notice
2. 2 months to respond
3. Company dissolved if no response

---

## LLP Confirmation Statement

Same requirements apply to LLPs:
- Annual confirmation statement
- Confirm members (not shareholders)
- Confirm PSC information
- Same fees and deadlines

---

## Exempt from PSC

Some entities exempt from PSC register:
- Companies with shares on regulated market
- Some subsidiaries of listed companies
