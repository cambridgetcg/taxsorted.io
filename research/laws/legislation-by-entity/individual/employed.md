## Key Difference from Self-Employed

| Aspect | Employed | Self-Employed |
|--------|----------|---------------|
| **Tax Collection** | PAYE (employer deducts) | Self Assessment |
| **NI Class** | Class 1 (employee) | Class 2 + 4 |
| **Expenses** | Very limited | Wholly & exclusively |
| **Trading Allowance** | N/A | £1,000 |
| **SA Required?** | Only if additional income | Always |

## When Self Assessment Required

An employed individual MUST file SA if:

| Condition | Threshold | Source |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Untaxed income | >£2,500 | TMA 1970 s.7 |
| Self-employment income | Any | TMA 1970 s.7 |
| Partnership income | Any | TMA 1970 s.12AA |
| Company director | Any (usually) | TMA 1970 s.7 |
| Total income | >£150,000 | TMA 1970 s.7 |
| Child Benefit claimant | Earning >£60,000 | FA 2012 |
| CGT disposal | Above annual exempt | TCGA 1992 |
| Foreign income | Any | ITA 2007 |

## Employment Status Tests

### Determining Employment vs Self-Employment

Source: Case law + HMRC Employment Status Manual (ESM)

| Factor | Employment | Self-Employment |
|--------|------------|-----------------|
| **Control** | Employer controls how/when/where | Worker controls own work |
| **Mutuality of Obligation** | Employer must provide work | No ongoing obligation |
| **Personal Service** | Must do work personally | Can substitute |
| **Financial Risk** | No risk; fixed pay | Risk of loss |
| **Equipment** | Employer provides | Worker provides |
| **Part of Business** | Integrated into employer | In business on own account |

**Key Cases**:
- Ready Mixed Concrete v Minister of Pensions [1968]
- Market Investigations v Minister of Social Security [1969]
- Hall v Lorimer [1994]

## Employment Expenses (Limited)

Unlike self-employed, employees can only claim:

| Expense | Condition | Source |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| **Travel** | Not ordinary commuting | ITEPA 2003 s.337-342 |
| **Professional fees** | On HMRC list | ITEPA 2003 s.343 |
| **Uniform/tools** | Required for job | ITEPA 2003 s.336 |
| **Working from home** | Required by employer | ITEPA 2003 s.336 |

**Key Rule**: Must be "wholly, exclusively and necessarily" for employment duties (ITEPA 2003 s.336) - stricter than self-employed "wholly and exclusively".
