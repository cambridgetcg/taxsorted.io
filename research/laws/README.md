## Overview

UK tax law derives from multiple sources in a clear hierarchy:

```
PRIMARY LEGISLATION (Acts of Parliament)
     │
     ├── Finance Acts (annual tax changes)
     ├── Consolidating Acts (ITA 2007, CTA 2009/2010, TCGA 1992)
     └── Enabling Acts (Companies Act 2006, Charities Act 2011)
           │
           ▼
SECONDARY LEGISLATION (Statutory Instruments)
     │
     ├── Treasury Orders
     ├── HMRC Regulations
     └── Commencement Orders
           │
           ▼
HMRC INTERPRETATION (NOT binding law)
     │
     ├── HMRC Internal Manuals
     ├── Statements of Practice
     ├── Extra-Statutory Concessions
     └── Guidelines for Compliance
           │
           ▼
CASE LAW (Judicial decisions)
     │
     ├── First-tier Tribunal (Tax)
     ├── Upper Tribunal (Tax and Chancery)
     ├── Court of Appeal
     └── Supreme Court
```

## The Finance Act Cycle

Each year, the UK enacts (usually) one or more Finance Acts:

| Stage | Timing | Effect |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **Budget** | Autumn/Spring | Chancellor announces changes |
| **PCTA Resolution** | Budget day | Some changes immediate effect |
| **Finance Bill** | ~7 days after | Detailed legislation introduced |
| **Commons Stages** | 2-3 months | Debate, committee, amendments |
| **Lords Stages** | ~1 week | Formality (no amendment power) |
| **Royal Assent** | ~3-4 months total | Becomes Finance Act [Year] |

> **Full detail**: See [Legislative Process](./finance-acts/legislative-process.md)

## Consolidated Tax Acts

These are the "base" legislation that Finance Acts amend:

| Act | Covers | Key Sections |
|-----|--------|--------------|
| **Income Tax Act 2007** | Income tax rules | Rates, allowances, reliefs |
| **Corporation Tax Act 2009** | CT income computation | Trading income, loan relationships |
| **Corporation Tax Act 2010** | CT structure | Rates, groups, reliefs |
| **TCGA 1992** | Capital gains tax | Disposals, computation, reliefs |
| **IHTA 1984** | Inheritance tax | Rates, exemptions, trusts |
| **VATA 1994** | Value Added Tax | Registration, rates, exemptions |
| **TMA 1970** | Tax administration | Returns, enquiries, penalties |
