## Master Chronological Timeline

### 16th Century

| Year | Legislation | Category | Key Change |
|------|-------------|----------|------------|
| 1535 | Statute of Uses | Trust | Attempted to abolish "uses" - led to modern trust |
| 1540 | Statute of Wills | Trust | Allowed testamentary disposition of land |

### 17th Century

| Year | Legislation | Category | Key Change |
|------|-------------|----------|------------|
| 1601 | Charitable Uses Act | Charity | First codification of charitable purposes |

### 18th Century

| Year | Legislation | Category | Key Change |
|------|-------------|----------|------------|
| 1775 | (First Building Society) | Mutual | Ketley's Building Society, Birmingham |
| 1793 | Rose's Act | Friendly Society | First recognition of friendly societies |
| 1799 | Income Tax Act | Individual | First UK income tax (10% on >£200) |

### 19th Century

| Year | Legislation | Category | Key Change |
|------|-------------|----------|------------|
| 1803 | Income Tax Act | Individual | Schedules A-E; deduction at source |
| 1816 | Repeal | Individual | Income tax abolished (restored 1842) |
| 1836 | Regulation of Benefit Building Societies Act | Building Society | First building society legislation |
| 1837 | Wills Act | Trust | Modern will requirements |
| 1842 | Income Tax Act 1842 | Individual | Permanent income tax at 7d in £ |
| 1844 | Joint Stock Companies Act | Company | Incorporation by registration |
| 1852 | Industrial and Provident Societies Act | Cooperative | First cooperative legislation |
| 1855 | Limited Liability Act | Company | Shareholder liability limited |
| 1862 | Companies Act 1862 | Company | Consolidated company law framework |
| 1874 | Building Societies Act | Building Society | Comprehensive framework |
| 1875 | Friendly Societies Act | Friendly Society | Comprehensive framework |
| 1888 | Mortmain and Charitable Uses Act | Charity | Replaced 1601 Act |
| 1890 | Partnership Act 1890 | Partnership | Codified partnership law (s.4(2) Scottish personality) |
| 1893 | Trustee Act 1893 | Trust | Trustee powers and duties |
| 1896 | Friendly Societies Act | Friendly Society | Consolidation |

|------|-------------|----------|------------|

| 1925 | Law of Property Act 1925 | Trust | Trusts of land provisions |
| 1946 | National Insurance Act 1946 | NI | Universal coverage |
| 1948 | Companies Act 1948 | Company | Post-war modernisation |
